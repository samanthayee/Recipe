Banana Bread

Delicious homestyle banana bread with a hint of walnuts for added decadance.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/8 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 cup Butter
  • 1/2 or 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Bananas (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1/2 cup Walnuts (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Soften butter to room temperature, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add milk, eggs and bananas seperately into the butter mixture and mix well after each addition. This is your wet bowl.
  4. In a seperate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt, mix until well distributed. This is your dry bowl.
  5. Pour 1/3 of the dry ingredients into the wet bowl and mix until incorportation, repeat with the other 2/3 of the dry ingredients, and mix until all is incorporated well.
  6. Add walnuts into batter if desired.
  7. Pour into loaf pan and bake for 55 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Let the loaf cool on a wire rack then proceed to cutting and serving.
